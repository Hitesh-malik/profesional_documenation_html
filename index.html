Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the documentation updated?

Our documentation is updated continuously. Major updates occur monthly with new features and improvements, while minor updates and corrections happen weekly. Each document displays its last update timestamp, allowing you to track the most recent changes.

Can I contribute to the documentation?

Yes, we welcome community contributions! You can suggest edits, report issues, or submit new content through our GitHub repository. All contributions are reviewed by our documentation team before being published to ensure accuracy and consistency.

Is there a difference between free and paid documentation?

All our core documentation is freely available to everyone. Premium documentation includes advanced topics, in-depth case studies, and specialized enterprise solutions. Enterprise customers also receive customized documentation, private knowledge bases, and priority support.

How do I request documentation for a specific topic?

You can request documentation on specific topics through our GitHub issues or community forum. Our team reviews all requests and prioritizes them based on community interest and strategic importance. You can also participate in our quarterly documentation planning sessions.
